Notes

This work appears in the online PDF catalogue for Ron English: Brand Royalty, available here, accessed April 12, 2026.

This composition was later reproduced by the artist as a related mixed-media limited edition on canvas released through Castle Fine Art in 2024. The listing gives the edition as 95, the framed size as 61.61 × 31.69 inches (156.49 × 80.49 cm), and notes that the work is hand-signed by Ron English. The page also states that it can be purchased in a set of two; see the related print on Castle Fine Art.

This work references Pablo Picasso’s Guernica.
