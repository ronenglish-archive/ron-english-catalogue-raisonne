Notes

This composition was later adapted by the artist as a related lithograph issued in 2015. The print measured 87 × 60.50 cm (34.25 × 23.81 inches), was produced on White BFK Rives 270 g paper as a 6-color lithograph printed with Marinoni lithographic machines and hand cut, and was released in an edition of 99. The print was signed and numbered by the artist; see the related print on Print Them All.
