Notes

According to the printed POWER PATHOS exhibition catalogue, Ron English worked at the Station Museum in the weeks prior to the opening to produce this painting.

This composition was later adapted by the artist as a related giclée print on Somerset Enhanced Radiant White Satin 330gsm paper issued as Grade School Guernica.

The image was also issued as a postcard by the Museo de la Paz de Gernika shop in connection with the 2017 exhibition, and was later used for VeVe’s Grade School Guernica 2022 digital collectible.

In Original Grin, this work was erroneously reported as 12 × 27 inches.

The composition references Pablo Picasso’s Guernica.
