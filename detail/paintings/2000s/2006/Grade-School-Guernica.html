Exhibitions

POWER PATHOS, Station Museum of Contemporary Art, Houston, Texas, US, June 21–September 24, 2006.

Notes

Reproduced in Allouche Gallery’s exhibition catalogue PDF for Ron English: Guernica, accessed April 5, 2026, https://www.allouchegallery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Ron-English-Guernica-Catalogue.pdf.

The work is documented in the Museo de la Paz de Gernika’s digital exhibition catalogue / flip-book, accessed April 5, 2026.

The work is reproduced and discussed in the printed Power Pathos exhibition catalogue. In Ron English’s section, the catalogue states that, in the weeks prior to the opening of the exhibition, he worked at the Station Museum to produce this painting.

In Original Grin, this work was erroneously reported as 12x27 inches.

This work references Pablo Picasso’s Guernica.
